Monkey Vape

/ VIEW CREDITS

Monkey Vape is a specialist e-cigarette store that wants to modernize its brand image and offer a site to sell its products in France.

/ TAG: New Atmosphère / CLIENT: Sandwich Design
/ MISSION

Monkey Vape wanted to assert a modern, innovative brand image by drawing inspiration from current trends, focusing is image on a new product and transform is ageing appearance.

The design af the scenography is sober, departing from conventional web design, we embraced a minimalist and premium aesthetic, mirroring the sophistication of the vape shop. This approach ensures a seamless and visually engaging experience for visitors, reflecting the commitment to simplicity. Every aspect, from layout to color palette, is meticulously curated to convey the vape shop's unique narrative.
